About Me

It is cliché to say that inquisitiveness is the common link among those interested in IT and entrepreneurial exploration. Cliché as it may be, I unashamedly lay claim to the reality that inquisitiveness has been the driving force in all my endeavours to date. Whether it was my school boy passion for cricket, my adolescent interest in cars and motorcycles or my university focus on IT and cybersecurity, tinkering, learning about the mechanics and imagining improvements to all three, have always claimed my passion to create, understand and improve myself and the focus of my attention. I’ve never done anything half-heartedly. Hours on the cricket pitch, in the gym and under the tutelage of coaches aimed at improving my game and my understanding of the game. Ditto regarding my automobile/motorcycle hobby. And now I’ve applied that same singular pursuit of knowledge and improvement to computer science, my university engineering chosen major. Through my university course, internships and entrepreneurial tinkering, I have developed a passion for open source development focusing my short-term goals on creating a private server to host family and friends while building a computer to learn loT and home automation. My long-term goal is to create and maintain NFC android app as a personal pet project and become a cybersecurity expert, utilising my sharpened public speaking skills, perseverance and time management abilities, to market my creations either through a start up or as part of a Big Tech company and hopefully lead a Big Tech Company.
